Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a staple from asian cuisine that you can enjoy at home in a matter of minutes.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Egg
  • Spring onion
  • Chilli
  • Chicken rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Asian sesame oil
  • Pork lard or peanut oil
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Oil the wok with pork lard/peanut oil
  2. Fry garlic and shallot until fragant
  3. Add egg, fry until fragant
  4. Add rice, stir
  5. Add sou sauce, sesame oil, MSG, stir
  6. Add spring onion, chilli
  7. Toss
