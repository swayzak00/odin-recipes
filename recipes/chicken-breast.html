Chicken Breast

Description

Fried chicken is an excellent and low fat source of protein you should include often in your meals

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pork lard

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the chicken breasts
  2. Distribute salt and pepper evenly using your fingertips
  3. Heat up frying pan and add pork lard
  4. Add chicken breasts to frying pan, flip until evenly cooked
